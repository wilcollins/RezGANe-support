Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2025

Overview

RezGANe is a macOS Finder extension for AI image upscaling. It is designed with privacy as a core principle — all processing happens entirely on your device.

Data Collection

RezGANe does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is included in the app.

Image Processing

All image upscaling is performed locally on your Mac using CoreML. Your images are never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party. No network connection is required to use RezGANe.

Third-Party Services

RezGANe does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be submitted via GitHub Issues.
